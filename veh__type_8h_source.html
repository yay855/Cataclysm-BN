81/* Flag info:
82 * INTERNAL - Can be mounted inside other parts
83 * ANCHOR_POINT - Allows secure seatbelt attachment
84 * OVER - Can be mounted over other parts
85 * MOUNTABLE - Usable as a point to fire a mountable weapon from.
86 * E_COLD_START - Cold weather makes the engine take longer to start
87 * E_STARTS_INSTANTLY - The engine takes no time to start, like foot pedals
88 * E_ALTERNATOR - The engine can mount and power an alternator
89 * E_COMBUSTION - The engine burns fuel to provide power and can burn or explode
90 * E_HIGHER_SKILL - Multiple engines with this flag are harder to install
91 * Other flags are self-explanatory in their names. */
