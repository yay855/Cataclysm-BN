81// Beer-Lambert law says attenuation is going to be equal to
82// 1 / (e^al) where a = coefficient of absorption and l = length.
83// Factoring out length, we get 1 / (e^((a1*a2*a3*...*an)*l))
84// We merge all of the absorption values by taking their cumulative average.
