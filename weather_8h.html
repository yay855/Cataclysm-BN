BODYTEMP

Body temperature.

Body temperature is measured on a scale of 0u to 10000u, where 10u = 0.02C and 5000u is 37C Outdoor temperature uses similar numbers, but on a different scale: 2200u = 22C, where 10u = 0.1C. Most values can be changed with no impact on calculations. Maximum heat cannot pass 15000u, otherwise the player will vomit to death.
