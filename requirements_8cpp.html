Helper function for deduped_requirement_data constructor below.

The goal of this function is to consolidate a particular item_comp that would otherwise be duplicated between two requirements.

It operates recursively (increasing index with the depth of recursion), searching for another item_comp to merge leftover with. For each compatible item_comp found it performs that merger and writes out a suitably updated form of the overall requirements to result.

If it chooses not to merge with any particular item_comp, then it deletes that item_comp from the options, to avoid the duplication.

Lastly, it also outputs a version of the requirements where leftover remains where it was, and all other compatible item_comp entries have been deleted.

Parameters
leftoverThe item_comp needing to be dealt with.
req_prefixThe requirements considered so far; more will be appended to this.
to_expandThe original requirements we are working through to look for a duplicate.
orig_indexThe index into the alter_item_comp_vector where leftover was originally to be found. If it isn't merged with another item, then it will be re-inserted at this position.
indexThe position within to_expand where we will next look for duplicates of leftover to merge with.
resultThe finished requirements should be appended to this.
